# TraceRay

## Description

Traces a ray through the acceleration structure.

#### glsl
Available in stages: `raygen`, `closesthit`, `miss`.

#### cuda
Available in stages: `raygen`, `closesthit`, `miss`.

#### spirv
Available in stages: `raygen`, `closesthit`, `miss`.

Requires capability: `spvRayTracingKHR`.
